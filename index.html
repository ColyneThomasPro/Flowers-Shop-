About Us

Welcome to Petals, where floral dreams come to life. Established in the heart of New York City in 2005, we embarked on a journey to bring the beauty of nature into the lives of our customers.
Our passion for flowers an dedication to quality craftsmanship have made Petals a beloved part of the community. From enchanting wedding bouquets to heartfelt sympathy arrangements, we have been honored to be a part of your most meaningful moments.
Over the years, we've cultivated relationships with local growers, ensuring that each s we use is a testament to freshness and vibrant beauty. Our team of skilled florists combines artistry with nature, creating arrangements that evoke emotions and leave lasting impressions.
As we continue to bloom and grow, we remain committed to providing our customers with the finest floral experiences. Thank you for choosing Petals—we look forward to being your trusted florist for years to come.
